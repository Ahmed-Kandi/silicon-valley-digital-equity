Why It Matters

We're pre-launch, targeting our first 5–10 builds in Summer 2026. Early support helps us establish the pipeline and prove the model.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

What condition do donated computers need to be in?

We accept machines in functional or repairable condition. Cosmetic wear is fine, we care about core components working. If you're unsure, reach out and we'll let you know if the machine is a good fit.

How old can donated machines be?

We typically work with machines from the past 8–10 years. Older hardware can struggle with modern software requirements. If you have machines outside this window, feel free to ask, we'll assess on a case-by-case basis.

What happens to our company's data?

All drives are securely wiped using a multi-pass process before any software is installed. We can provide documentation of the wipe upon request. Your data security is a priority.

Is my monetary donation really tax-deductible?

We are pursuing fiscal sponsorship through Hack Club Bank, a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Once that approval is confirmed, all monetary donations will be tax-deductible. We will update this page when the sponsorship is active.

How do families receive computers?

We are pursuing a partnership with Second Harvest of Silicon Valley to refer families through their existing network. This partnership is pending confirmation.

Can our company volunteer or partner with SVDE?

Absolutely. We welcome partnerships of all kinds, from hardware donations to technical volunteers who can help with refurbishment. Reach out via our contact form and we'll find a way to work together.
